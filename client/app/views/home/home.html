Learn new words with fun !
Kidkards helps children to learn new words in English. They can explore words by categories, listen to their pronunciations or match with their related pictures.

Kidkards is available on Apple iOS Devices. Check it out and Download : Click Here

Explore the cards

Children can look through all the cards by selecting a category
They can learn new words, expand each card and discover new objects.

Practice

Children can practice their knowledges by trying to remember each card name.
They can touch the navigation buttons or swipe to change the card.
If they touch the card, it will flip reveiling the word. A voice will also say the word to help them remember and to pronounce it. This feature can be turned off by touching the sound icon.

Play

Children can play a matching card game, helping them to remember the cards while having fun!
